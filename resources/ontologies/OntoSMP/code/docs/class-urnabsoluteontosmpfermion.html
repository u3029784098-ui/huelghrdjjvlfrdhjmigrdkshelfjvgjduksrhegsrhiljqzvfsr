URI

urn:absolute:OntoSMP#Fermion

Label

Fermion

Description

Fermions are particles with half-integer spin, such as ½ or 3/2. They obey the Pauli exclusion principle, which means that no two identical fermions can occupy the same quantum state. This property is crucial for the structure of matter. Fermions include quarks and leptons. They are the building blocks of matter, with their behavior determining the structure of atoms and molecules.
